Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil or ghee
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • 4 cups water
  • Whole spices (cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon)

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pot, add whole spices, and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add onions and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sauté for 2 minutes.
  5. Add chicken and cook until white.
  6. Stir in tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt; cook until tomatoes soften.
  7. Add yogurt, mint, and cilantro; cook for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Stir in rice gently, then add water and bring to a boil.
  9. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  10. Fluff with a fork and serve hot.

Pasta Primavera

Ingredients

  • 200 grams pasta (your choice)
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add bell pepper, zucchini, and carrot; cook until tender.
  4. Add cooked pasta, salt, and pepper; toss to combine.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, cream together butter, sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well.
  4. In another bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually mix into wet ingredients.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
