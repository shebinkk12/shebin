Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil or ghee
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • 4 cups water
  • Whole spices (2-3 cloves, 2-3 cardamom pods, 1 stick cinnamon)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak for 30 minutes and drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat the oil or ghee. Add the whole spices and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  6. Stir in chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  7. Add yogurt, mint, and cilantro. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Add soaked rice to the chicken mixture and stir gently.
  9. Pour in 4 cups of water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes or until rice is cooked.
  10. Once done, sprinkle garam masala on top. Fluff the biryani gently with a fork.
  11. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Enjoy your Chicken Biryani!
